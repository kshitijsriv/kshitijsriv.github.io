About me

Hi! I’m Kshitij. I’m working as a Research Assistant with Dr Pravesh Biyani at the Mobility and Optimisation Lab at IIIT-Delhi.

Research Interest

I have been making additions to Open Transit Data, Delhi, helping with GTFS data for public transit buses in Delhi for the platform. My work and projects can be viewed here.
